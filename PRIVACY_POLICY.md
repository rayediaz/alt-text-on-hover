# Privacy Policy

Alt Text Hover Inspector does not collect, transmit, or sell personal data.

## Data access
- The extension reads image elements on the current page to display alt text and produce a local scan report.
- Page data is processed locally in your browser and is not sent to any server.

## Stored data
- The extension stores a single preference (enabled/disabled) in Chrome sync storage to keep your toggle setting consistent across browsers.

## Third parties
- The extension does not use third-party analytics or tracking.

## Contact
- If you have questions about this policy, contact the developer through the Chrome Web Store listing.
